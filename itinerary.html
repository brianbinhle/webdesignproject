Seven Day Itinerary


Hey everyone! I'm super excited to share the detailed plan for my upcoming seven-day trip to Japan. This itinerary is packed with everything from adrenaline-pumping activities to serene moments of relaxation, all centered around the majestic Mount Fuji. Here's a breakdown of what each day looks like, with links to dive deeper into the details of each adventure. Let's get started!

  • Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation in Kawaguchiko: Arrive and unwind by the tranquil Lake Kawaguchi.
  • Day 2: Fuji-Q Highland Thrills and Sake Tasting: Enjoy thrilling rides and sample local sake.
  • Day 3: Cultural Immersion in Fujiyoshida: Explore iconic cultural sites and traditional cuisine.
  • Day 4: Outdoor Adventure in the Fuji Five Lakes Area: Cycle through scenic trails and enjoy a lakeside picnic.
  • Day 5: Hiking and Nature in Aokigahara Forest: Discover the unique ecosystem and folklore of Aokigahara.
  • Day 6: Scenic Views and Art in Hakone: Visit art museums and enjoy a boat cruise on Lake Ashi.
  • Day 7: Departure Day with Culinary Delights: Savor a final meal with views of Mount Fuji before departing.
