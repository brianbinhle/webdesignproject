Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation in Kawaguchiko


Morning

Arrive at Narita Airport and take a private transfer to the Fuji region. Check into your accommodation and enjoy the serene views of Lake Kawaguchi.

Afternoon

For lunch, savor the local specialty at Houtou Fudou, known for its hearty noodle hot pot. Afterward, visit the Kawaguchiko Music Forest Museum to immerse yourself in a world of music boxes.

Evening

In the evening, unwind at Fujiyama Onsen, a traditional hot spring with a view of Mount Fuji. Relax and rejuvenate after your journey.
